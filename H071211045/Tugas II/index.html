Our Latest Projects

Autonomous Rover Drone

This project is a continuation from earlier mars landing research. XB2 Rover drones will circle every area that has never been entered before.

Eveniet Space Lift

A Space lift that can carry 10000 tons of cargo to the space. The lift can withstand the pressure of the space and the velocity of Earth's rotation.

New Guinea Floating Island

Guinea hyper drive is a new technology that can create a floating island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. The island can be used as a base for the colonization of Mars.

The First Martian City

Martian City of Mars is a city that is built on the surface of Mars. The city is built with the latest technology and is equipped with everything needed for human life.

Mars Terraforming

The Mars Terraforming Project is a project to make Mars suitable for human life. The project is still in the research phase and is expected to be completed in 2030.
